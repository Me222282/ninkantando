Ninkantando is an offical company founded by the Makio and Linguini corporation. It was initially created to defeat the evil Balgar, but that endeavour seemed to go nowhere. The board of dictators then decided to open Makio and Linguini's Fun Kingdom, which was a resounding success. With popularity growing exponentially, Makio and Linguini Productions was created to produce the first (and last) Makio and Linguini themed film, Mega Bang Brothers.
